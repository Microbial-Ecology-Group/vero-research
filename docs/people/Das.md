## Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the Veterinary Education, Research & Outreach (VERO) Program at Texas A&M University, working in Dr. Paul Morley's research group. I completed my PhD in Quantitative Biology at the University of Texas at Arlington. Prior to that, I earned a Master of Science in Botany from the University of Kalyani (West Bengal, India), followed by a Master of Technology in Agricultural Systems and Management from the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (IIT Kharagpur, India).
